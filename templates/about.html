Where it all started

Around five years ago I was approached to teach at a local cooking class which I jumped to the opportunity to be apart of. Since then I've taught hundreds of people new and exciting ways to cook through The Online Cooking Guide and as a guest to cooking classes taken place across Ireland and the UK. We have a real passion for cooking and learning new ways to cook and we ultimately we want to train our students to be the best cooks they can be as well. We are on a range of social networking platforms; Facebook, Instagram and Pinterest, and are hoping to get into the world of TikTok soon! We hope you enjoy learning how to cook with us and why not check out some of our free recipes before buying one of our packages!
- Happy Cooking!
